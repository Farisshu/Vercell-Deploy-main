# Technical Terms - Japanese for Engineers 🇯🇵

## Overview

Essential Japanese technical terms for embedded systems engineers working in Japan. This guide includes English, Japanese (Kanji + Kana), Romaji, and meanings.

## Kanji Practice: Essential Characters

### 電 (den) - Electricity
- 電気 (denki) - electricity
- 電子 (denshi) - electron/electronic
- 電池 (denchi) - battery

### 気 (ki) - Spirit/Energy
- 電気 (denki) - electricity
- 空気 (kūki) - air
- 気持ち (kimochi) - feeling

### 車 (sha/kuruma) - Vehicle
- 自動車 (jidōsha) - automobile
- 電車 (densha) - train
- 車両 (sharyō) - rolling stock

### 製 (sei) - Manufacture
- 製品 (seihin) - product
- 製造 (seizō) - manufacturing
- 製作 (seisaku) - production

## Pronunciation Tips

1. **Long vowels**: Extend the sound (ō = o-o, ē = e-e)
2. **Double consonants**: Brief pause (kk, tt, pp)
3. **N (ん)**: Nasal sound, changes based on next letter
4. **Pitch accent**: Important but don't worry too much initially
